## üí° PRO TIPS FOR FAMILY CAMPING

1. **Test Gear at Home:** Set up tent in backyard before trip. Practice with kids.

2. **Packing Cubes by Person:** Each family member gets a different color cube. Easy to find items.

3. **Wet Bag:** Bring extra plastic bags for wet/muddy clothes after hikes. Keeps clean clothes clean.

4. **Glow Sticks:** Give kids glow sticks at night. Makes them visible and entertains them.

5. **Campfire Stories:** Research local indigenous stories or bring story cards. Memorable family time.

6. **Early Starts:** Best wildlife viewing and photos are sunrise. Worth waking kids early for spectacular mornings.

7. **Junior Ranger Program:** Parks Canada has kids programs. Educational and fun activity booklets.

8. **Scavenger Hunt:** Create nature scavenger hunt list for kids. Keeps them engaged on hikes.

9. **Star Gazing:** Dark skies in mountains = amazing stars. Download star app, teach kids constellations.

10. **Backup Plans:** Have rainy day activities ready (drive to hot springs, visit town museums, card games in tent).
