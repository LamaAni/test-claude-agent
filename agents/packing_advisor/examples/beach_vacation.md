## ğŸ’¡ PRO TIPS

1. **Mid-Trip Laundry:** Pack 5 days of clothes, wash on Day 5-6, rewear. This saves 3-4 kg vs. packing 10 days of clothes.

2. **Sarong Multi-Use:** A sarong serves as beach cover-up, temple wrap, blanket, towel, and scarf. Very versatile!

3. **Reef-Safe Sunscreen:** Bali is working to protect coral reefs. Bring reef-safe sunscreen or buy locally.

4. **Swim Before Breakfast:** Beaches are calmest and least crowded early morning.

5. **Temple Timing:** Visit temples early morning or late afternoon (cooler, better light for photos, fewer crowds).

6. **Waterproof Your Valuables:** Use dry bag for phone/wallet/camera when on boats or near water.

7. **Pack a Light Layer:** Indoor AC can be intense (malls, restaurants, flights). Light cardigan or hoodie useful.

8. **Bring Small Bills:** For tipping, small purchases at markets. Break large bills at hotels/restaurants.

9. **Photo Backups:** Back up photos daily (cloud or second device). Humidity can damage electronics.

10. **Flexibility:** Bali runs on "island time" - things may not go exactly on schedule. Relax and enjoy!
